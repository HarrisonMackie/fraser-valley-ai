About Fraser Valley AI

Fraser Valley AI is dedicated to demystifying artificial intelligence for everyday users and businesses. Our mission is to make AI accessible, useful, and ethical. Based in British Columbia, we support local entrepreneurs and professionals with hands-on training, practical examples, and ongoing guidance.

What We Teach

Our curriculum is designed for beginners and non-technical audiences. Topics include:

  • Foundations of AI: History, terminology, and how modern AI systems work.
  • Practical AI Tools: ChatGPT and other chatbots, text summarization, content generation, translation, and image creation tools.
  • Prompt Engineering: How to craft effective prompts to get the best results from AI models.
  • AI for Business: Using AI for marketing, customer support, data analysis, workflow automation, and creative tasks.
  • Building Workflows: Integrating multiple AI tools into repeatable processes that save time and resources.
  • Data Privacy & Ethics: Understanding the limitations of AI, responsible use, and protecting sensitive information.
